# STRIDE Reference

## Spoofing

- Identity impersonation due to weak authentication.

## Tampering

- Unauthorized modification of code, data, or configuration.

## Repudiation

- Inability to prove who performed sensitive operations.

## Information Disclosure

- Exposure of confidential data through responses, logs, backups, or transport.

## Denial of Service

- Resource exhaustion or algorithmic complexity abuse.

## Elevation of Privilege

- Gaining higher privileges through broken authz or unsafe trust assumptions.

## Trust Boundary Checklist

- Internet to edge service.
- Service to service.
- Service to database.
- Runtime to OS or shell.
